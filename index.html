Welcome to X, Simplified.

X, Simplified is a passion project which intends to explore the benefits of coding to anyone unfamiliar with the subject. Non-STEM students are taught how to use coding for their tasks without diving too deep into unrelated computer science concepts. Moreover, it assists kids in starting their coding journey.

Each intakes are split into sessions, whereby a number of students are selected. We work with these students, teaching them the ropes and promoting collaboration every step of the way.
